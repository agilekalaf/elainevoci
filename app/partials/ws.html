Each quarter, I facilitate life skills workshops designed to help clients develop the emotional and psychological resources and strengths to meet life’s challenges; enable them to make better choices that result in success and happiness; and to provide greater self-awareness, understanding and knowledge of the unique gifts each brought into this life. These workshops have helped people discover and use their personal power to free themselves from the past in order to reinvent themselves and build a self-image that more closely aligns with the self they experience.

Ideal Participant:

If you seek to know yourself better, to heal painful life experiences, and/or to reinvent yourself in order to find a new career, begin a new relationship, or create a new outlook on life, then these workshops can be a great source of inspiration, new energy and enjoyment.

  • Signature Workshops – each of these classes stands alone and is offered regularly; check the website for dates and times. The fees are listed below.
  • Death Cafes - A series of monthly gatherings to increase the awareness of death with a view to helping people make the most of their lives.
  • Successfully Managing Personal Transitions - a three hour workshop to help you make the most of change.  Change happens when something starts or stops in our lives; transition is the psychological process we must go through to honor the past and become oriented to the future.  $60 per person.
  • Spirituality and Aging - Five spiritual practices for creating a happy second half of life.  Learn what makes for healthy aging, and how to perfect the art of life completion.  $60 per person.
  • Creating the Work You Love – a three hour workshop that provides clarity, vision, facts, inspiration and – most importantly – an opportunity for you to explore your heart’s true desires regarding your career in a small group experience. Fee is $60.
