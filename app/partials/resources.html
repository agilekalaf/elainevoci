Reading List and Web Resources
There are many excellent books to help enrich and support you on your journey. These are books that I have found personally helpful and often recommend to clients in my coaching practice. I grouped them into categories and some of them could easily fit into all four.


TO INSPIRE YOU

  • Gift from the Sea by Anne Morrow Lindbergh
  • Kitchen Table Wisdom by Rachel Naomi Remen
  • This Time I Dance! Creating the Work You Love by Tama Kieves
  • The Invitation by Oriah Mountain Dreamer
  • Do What You Love and the Money Will Follow by Marsha Sinetar
  • Eat, Pray, Love by Elizabeth Gilbert
  • Bridgebuilders: Ordinary Women Doing Extraordinary Things by Elaine Voci


TO AWAKEN YOU


  • Way of the Peaceful Warrier by Dan Millman
  • Wherever You Go There You Are by Jon Kabat-Zinn
  • Move Your Stuff, Change Your Life by Karen Rauch Carter
  • The Intuitive Way: the definitive guide to increasing your awareness by Penny Pierce
  • Your Owner’s Manual by Burt Hotchkiss
  • Meditations to Change Your Life by Louise Hay
  • My Stroke Of Insight, A Brain Scientist’s Personal Journey by Jill Bolte Taylor, Ph.D.
  • Excuse Me, Your Life is Waiting by Lynn Grabhorn
  • Lots of Candles, Plenty of Cake by Anna Quindlen
  • The Road Less Traveled by M. Scott Peck, M.D.
  • A New Earth by Eckhart Tolle
  • A Woman’s Journey to God: Finding the Feminine Path by Joan Borysenko


ON WORKING AND CAREER GOALS


  • What Color’s Your Parachute by Richard Bolles
  • The Girl’s Guide to Starting Your Own Business by Caitlin Friedman and Kimberly Yorio
    • To Build the Life You Want Create the Work You Love by Marsha Sinetar
    Do What You Are by Paul Tieger and Barbara Baron-Tieger
    The Complete Idiot’s Guide to The Perfect Resume by Susan Ireland
