Reading List
There are many excellent books to help enrich and support you on your journey. These are books that I have found personally helpful and often recommend to clients in my coaching practice.


FOR PERSONAL GROWTH

  • Frequency: the power of Personal Vibration, by Penney Peirce (free audio downloads with printed book)
  • The Gifts of Caregiving: Stories of Hardship, Hope and Healing by Connie Goldman
  • E2: Nine do-it-yourself energy experiments that prove your thoughts create your reality by Pam Grout
  • Writing to Change the World: an inspiring guide for transforming the world with words by Mary Pipher
  • When Things Fall Apart: Heart Advice for Difficult Times by Pema Chodron
  • Soul of a Citizen: Living with Conviction in Challenging Times by Paul Rogat Loeb


FOR INSPIRATION


  • Tattoos on the Heart by Father Gregory Boyle
  • A New Earth: Awakening to Your Life’s Purpose by Eckhart Tolle
  • The Last Lecture by Randy Pausch
  • Tuesdays with Morrie by Mitch Albom
  • Kitchen Table Wisdom by Naomi Naomi Remen
  • Mindful Meditation for Beginners by Jon Kabat-Zinn


FOR YOUR CAREER


  • This Time I Dance by Tama J. Kieves
  • What Color’s Your Parachute? by Richard Bolles
  • What Color’s Your Parachute: Job Hunters Workbook by Richard Bolles
  • What’s Next? By Kerry Hannon
  • Decisive: How To Make Better Choices In Life and Work by Chip Heath and Dan Heath
  • Quiet: The Power of Introverts in a World That Can’t Stop Talking by Susan Cain
  • Make a Name for Yourself by Robin Fisher Roffer
