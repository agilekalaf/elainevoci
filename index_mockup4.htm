Elaine Voci is a recognized life skills coach and writer whose work relates to issues of personal and professional growth. If it involves reinventing yourself, it involves her. She has coached, encouraged and helped hundreds of people who have learned how to improve communication skills, achieve a healthy life -work balance, and strengthened skills of intuition and creativity in finding their life’s work.

Elaine’s writing focuses on the everyday “hero” and is both inspirational and educational. Fascinated by how ordinary people deal with challenging circumstances, she writes about examples found in daily life that are compelling and instructive. She has published three nonfiction books, contributed to several books in business and cross –cultural communication, and authored numerous magazine articles. Her work has been published in the US, Japan, Canada and the UK.

An innovative trainer, she has created unique training programs including Creating the Work You Love, a half-day workshop for working adults that provides support for finding clarity, vision and the courage to create work that is soul satisfying. In addition, Elaine facilitates Intent Heals workshops that offer a tangible way for people to engage in prayer, gratitude and forgiveness using Intent Heals journals ™ made in Nepal.

Elaine’s philosophy of life deeply influences her life coaching, workshops and written work. She believes that service to others is the “rent you pay for your room on earth” (Mohammed Ali, 2010) and is an active volunteer in the community.
