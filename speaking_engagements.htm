An evening with...

Belonging to our Longing: The Poetry of Relationship with Liza Hyatt,poet, writer, adjunct professor, art therapist. Liza will speak about the importance of tending the poetic imagination in order to navigate and fully live our complex and hectic lives.

When: September 26, 2013 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM
Where: 11805 North Pennsylvania Street
Carmel, IN

The Heart Never Lies: Why Following Your Heart Through Life Makes Perfect Sense with Louise Dunn, Founder and Dean of Heartland Miracles, LLC. When in doubt, listen to your heart. That is the motto Louise uses to guide her through the decisions that have a major impact on her life and her work. From the halls of a Catholic convent in her teens to the entrepreneurial adventure of founding a school of forgiveness in her 60s, Louise will share her story of following heart-wisdom and soul whispers to success and happiness in both her personal and professional life.

When: October 24, 2013 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM
Where: 11805 North Pennsylvania Street
Carmel, IN

Creating Life Out of a Thin Soul: Renovate Your Life Journey by Finding Your Voice and Passion with Penny Dunning, Entrepreneur, Poet, Publisher. Learn how to renovate your life journey to lead to fulfillment and success by finding your voice and passion. Penny will share her story of how she recreated her life in her mid-forties and learned to sculpt it into exactly what she needed.

When: November 14, 2013 6:30 PM - 8:00 PM
Where: 11805 North Pennsylvania Street
Carmel, IN

Fee: $20 per person
