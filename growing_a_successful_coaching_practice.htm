How to Grow a Successful Coaching Practice

Each of us is meant to have a character all our own, to be what no other can exactly be, and do what no other can exactly do.
—William Channing

A three-hour interactive, fast-paced workshop designed for coaches to convert their passion and mission into a successful coaching practice and get paid for doing what they love.

Inspiring stories, marketing tools and techniques are included to help coaches make a connection and form lasting relationships that can transform lives. A step- by-step approach for implementing a winning marketing strategy that includes: A MemorableYou; Clarity of Message; Authenticity; Consistency and Intended Results. Participants will discover the value of a website and a Facebook page for their practice, and how to become masterful at niche marketing and selling. The workshop will help answer the key question: “Is coaching my business or a hobby?” Each participant will receive a binder of handouts and materials, including a list of helpful websites for future reference.
