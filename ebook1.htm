It’s here! My new e-book titled Creating the Work You Love: A Guide to Finding Your Right Livelihood has just been published.  It’s available in formats that include PDF (reading on PC/MAC); EPUB (iPad, Nook and most e-book readers) and/or  MOBI (Kindle format) and is priced at $6.95.
